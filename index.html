The Business Problem

Commercial teams relied on slow, manual Excel reports for tender performance across regions, leading to delayed decision-making and missed revenue opportunities.

The Technical Solution

  • Architected a Modern Data Lakehouse on AWS.
  • Built orchestrations in AWS Glue to ingest raw data from legacy systems into Amazon S3 (Bronze layer).
  • Utilized Databricks (PySpark) for complex transformations, data cleaning, and applying business logic (Silver layer).
  • modeled aggregated data in Amazon Redshift for high-performance serving to dashboards.

Results

  • Reduced reporting latency from weekly to daily.
